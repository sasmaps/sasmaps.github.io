About Us

Welcome to SAS Maps, your go-to source for exciting Minecraft parkour maps! Our team at SAS Maps is passionate about creating challenging and entertaining parkour experiences for Minecraft players of all skill levels.

Founded in 2022, SAS Maps has been dedicated to pushing the boundaries of parkour map design. We believe that parkour maps should not only test your skills but also provide a sense of adventure and creativity.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality and innovative parkour maps that bring joy and excitement to the Minecraft community. We strive to create maps that challenge players while offering a unique and enjoyable gaming experience.

Thank you for being a part of the SAS Maps community. Join us on our journey as we continue to explore and expand the world of Minecraft parkour! Our name SAS came from our first names (Skocko, AndreyHero, Spaghetti)
